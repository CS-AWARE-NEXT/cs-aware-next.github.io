The project consortium is comprised of the following organisations as project partners.

### [University of Oulu](https://www.oulu.fi)

The University of Oulu is a northern, international science university. Producing new scientific knowledge and solutions to build a more sustainable, intelligent and humane world.


### [CeRICT](https://www.cerict.it/en/)

CeRICT is a non-profit organization able to direct the results of research into application projects, realized jointly with the companies, such as to respond effectively to the needs of the ICT area, both nationally and internationally. CeRICT represents the natural evolution of the ICT Regional Competence Centre (CRdC ICT), which was started from an initiative of the Campania region at the end of 2002 in order to establish a network of skills on Information and Communication Technologies.


### [Politecnico di Milano](https://www.polimi.it/)

Since 1863, Italy's largest science-technology university, between scientific rigor and creativity.


### [Fujitsu Technology Solutions (Luxembourg)](https://www.fujitsu.com/lu/)

We believe that technology holds the key to building regenerative enterprises that thrive sustainably. By harnessing advanced solutions the power of artificial intelligence, cutting-edge cybersecurity, and application modernization, we empower businesses to not only achieve their goals but also contribute positively to the environment and society.


### [Innovative Secure Tehnologies](https://www.innosec.gr/en/)

InnoSec is a spin-off company, founded in 2014 in Thessaloniki and is related to the Alexander Technological Educational Institute (A.TEI) of Thessaloniki. In addition, the company itself pursues participation in both national and international research projects, so as to produce state-of-the-art research in the field of information security.


### [Wise & Munro](https://www.wisemunro.eu/)

Wise & Munro is a research and development company set in the governmental capital of the Netherlands, The Hague.

We are expert in the field of collaboration and collaborative learning & development, with and without technology. In recent projects, the field of cybersecurity is explored, with an emphasis on understanding cybersecurity from a social-technical viewpoint, focusing on increasing cybersecurity awareness of all people involved.

We engage in fundamental and applied research, often in a European context. Furthermore, we develop education and training and support innovation and change.


### [CeSViTer Consulting](https://www.cesviterconsulting.it/home/)

### [Open Technology Services](https://ots.gr/)

Open Technology Services (OTS) is an IT company, established in 1993, specialized in developing integrated information systems that fully meet the needs of Public and Private Sector.


### [Focus Europe](https://www.focuseurope.org/)

Focus Europe, founded in 2007, supports local authorities in accessing European funds, promoting territorial development.

With offices in Italy, Spain and Belgium, we facilitate dialogue with community institutions, promoting the planning, training and internationalization of SMEs. Since 2024 we have been a Third Sector Entity, known as FOCUS EUROPE ETS - THE NETWORK OF EUROPEAN TERRITORIES.

The associated institutions are represented in the Community institutions: at the European Parliament through information and training processes; at the European Commission through co-financing of pilot projects; at the partners of the Community Europe through programmatic comparisons on the management and valorization of territorial resources.

### [Osterreichische Computer Gesellschaft](https://www.ocg.at/)

The Austrian Computer Society (OCG) is a non-profit association for the promotion of information technology, taking into account its interactions with people and society. The association offers an interdisciplinary forum and is a dialogue partner for current and socio-politically relevant IT topics. Networking and promoting relations between science and business are important concerns. In addition, the OCG offers a standardised, independent and high-quality continuing education programme in the IT field and thus builds an important bridge to professional life.
